A quick guide to choosing a door

Tell us your priorities on a call and we'll narrow this list down fast — but here's what tends to matter most.

Climate & insulation

Attached garage, or a space you heat? Higher R-value cuts down on drafts, energy loss and noise through the winter.

Style & curb appeal

Carriage-house and wood-look doors tend to lift resale value; clean sectional or glass styles suit modern builds.

Budget

Sectional steel offers the best value per door; carriage-house, glass and custom doors sit at a higher price point.

Smart features

Most doors can pair with a smart opener for phone-based control, schedules and alerts — ask us during your quote.
